Rotational Molding

Rotational molding is a cost effective way to produce hollow parts such as liquid tanks or hollow containers.
World Class Prototypes has developed a proprietary system for producing rotational molded prototype parts at room temperature, as well as conventional oven curing. Here are some of the specific types of molding we do:

  • Molded inserts
  • Molded in color
  • Shore A and Shore D materials
  • Molded in texture
