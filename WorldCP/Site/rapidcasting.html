Rapid Casting

Urethane castings are a great way to generate prototype parts for trade shows, engineering and design proveouts, sales and marketing samples or short run pre-production parts. Urethanes have a wide variety of physical properties which can simulate most production materials for part evaluation. Urethanes simulate various production materials. Listed below are just some of the different rapid casting we do here at World Class Prototypes:

  • Shore A and D durometers
  • Clear or tinted lenses
  • Molded in color
  • Molded in textures
  • Molded inserts
  • Multiple shot materials
  • Undercuts or no draft parts
  • Silicone molds can produce over 50 parts per tool
  • Virtually any size part
  • Glass and other fiber filled resins
