Fiber Reinforced Composites

World Class Prototypes provides product design, tool design, and production applications in composite materials. World Class Prototypes has capabilities to produce tooling and parts from simple lay-ups to complex, including pre-pregs and vacuum bagging.
Here are some of our specialties in fiber reinforced composites that we do at World Class Prototypes:

  • Master molds/models
  • Prototype tools (plaster/fiberglass/carbon fiber)
  • Composite tooling
  • Compression tools
  • Bond tools
  • Nests and fixtures for assembly
