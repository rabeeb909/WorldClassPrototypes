Rapid Casting

Urethane castings are a great way to generate prototype parts for trade shows, engineering and design proveouts, sales and marketing samples or short run pre-production parts. Urethanes have a wide variety of physical properties which can simulate most production materials for part evaluation. Urethanes simulate various production materials.
